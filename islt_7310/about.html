Education Overview

Application Administrator – University of Missouri/Missouri Online (October 2021 – present)

As an administrator, I am part of the Canvas admins and my responsibilities include maintaining the learning management system, as well as being the primary administrator for Canvas Catalog, an Instructure product that allows us to list Canvas non-credit courses publicly so external users are able to enroll in them, and I am also leading the pilot for Impact, another Instructure product that allows us to display targeted messaging to users, as well as track tool adoption within Canvas.

Projects:

  • Impact rollout and pilot – Provide as lead admin, manage targeted communication requests and collect data on successful usage of tool and feedback of users about the tool
  • Canvas Catalog Administrator meetings – Lead Canvas Catalog meetings and inform subcatalog admins of upcoming changes and address issues.
  • Canvas integration upgrade – Work with team to upgrade current Canvas integrations from 1.1 to 1.3
